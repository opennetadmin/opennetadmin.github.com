I am not aware of any issues that would cause an impact to you if you are already using the those two build scripts. You should be just fine.

However, in saying that I would still suggest to you that you make a copy of your /opt/ona directory in case you want to revert back to what you had before. And also do a backup of your mysql database. I would always expect that any code that I give out has the chance of turning your data into dust, so take proper precautions. It should be a simple upgrade and a simple backup/restore type process though so I'd say go for it.

If you have any issues let me know.. I hang out on IRC in the #ona channel of freenode.net. Plus there are others there that could help as well.
